About Us

About us- Love Our City Klean (LOCK) is a creative waste management company that is focused on the effective diversion of waste from landfill for minimisation and repurposing. LOCK has been facilitating environmental education and awareness projects around the east of the City of Joburg for the eradication of illegal dumping hotspots and changing the look and feel of these hotspots to walls with murals that speak to “klean, safe and green”.
We have been spearheading separation at source which is the separation of waste into two categories (wet and dry) as we are still introducing this lifestyle we do not want to complicate the separating from household level in order to make it convenient for residents in our community to adopt it for the sole purpose of recovering valuable materials that are clean (high value) not contaminated for their diversion away from landfill to further sort and channel into the appropriate “afterlife”.
